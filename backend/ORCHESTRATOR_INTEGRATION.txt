================================================================================
ORCHESTRATOR LOGIC:
================================================================================

STEP 1: Base Score
- Uses credit_score_from_prob(model_probability)
- Generates score 300-900

STEP 2: Apply Penalties (Deterministic)
- Income mismatch >12% → -200
- Expense mismatch >20% → -150
- Trust score <0.6 → -120
- Fraud probability >0.4 → -250
- Missing required docs → -100
- Employment instability → -80
- Income stability <0.5 → -70
- Expense pattern <0.5 → -50

STEP 3: Risk Band
- Score ≥750 → LOW
- Score 650-749 → MEDIUM
- Score <650 → HIGH

STEP 4: Decision
- Fraud ≥0.6 → REJECTED
- Trust <0.3 → REJECTED
- HIGH risk → REJECTED
- MEDIUM risk → REVIEW
- LOW risk + pending docs → REVIEW
- LOW risk + fraud >0.3 → REVIEW
- Otherwise → APPROVED

STEP 5: Approval Probability
- (final_score - 300) / 600
- Always aligned with score

STEP 6: Consistency Rules
- Verification flags → cap at 94%
- Low trust (<0.5) → cannot be LOW risk, cap at 75%
- High fraud (>0.4) → cannot be APPROVED, cap at 65%
- Partial verification → cap at 88%
- Pending verification → cap at 80%, cannot be APPROVED
- Incomplete verification → cap at 70%, cannot be APPROVED
